We're here to help with your account, data, subscriptions, and questions about ANITA. Contact us by email or through the app.

How we can help

Subscriptions & payments

Billing, plan changes, cancellation, refunds. Payments are processed via Apple.

Data & privacy

Export or delete your data, privacy questions, GDPR/CCPA rights. You can also use Settings in the app to export, clear data, or delete your account.

Features & bugs

Questions about features, reporting bugs, or feedback.

Before you contact us

Check Settings in the app to export data, clear data, or delete your account. For subscriptions, you can also use your subscription settings in the App Store. If reporting a bug, include device type, OS version, and app version.
